# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — contributors and maintainers — pledge to make participation in IsoDAQ Studio
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and welcoming to newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments; personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the
maintainer at **sashok0127@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to
the circumstances. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct
may be removed from the project.
